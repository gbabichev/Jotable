How do I create a new note in Jotable?
Simply tap the "+" button or "New Note" button to create a fresh note. You can start typing immediately to add your thoughts, ideas, or any information you want to save.
How do I organize my notes with categories?
Jotable makes it easy to stay organized! Create categories to group related notes together. Just use the category management feature to create a new category and assign notes to it. This keeps your notes tidy and easy to find.
How do I use smart formatting in Jotable?
Jotable's smart formatting automatically helps you as you type. Just type a dash (-) to create bullet points, numbers (1., 2., etc.) for numbered lists, or use special characters for checkboxes. The app recognizes these patterns and formats them automatically ‚Äî no extra steps needed!
Can I customize the text appearance in my notes?
Yes! Jotable lets you personalize your notes with customizable text formatting. You can adjust font size, add bold, underline, strikethrough, and even change the text color to make your notes truly your own.
Is Jotable free to use?
Yes! Jotable is completely free with no hidden charges or in-app purchases. No AI, no ads ‚Äî just a clean, focused space for your thoughts, exactly as it should be.
Will my notes sync across my devices?
Absolutely! Jotable uses iCloud to keep your notes seamlessly synced across all your Apple devices ‚Äî iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Your notes stay up-to-date wherever you are.

üîß Troubleshooting

iCloud Sync Issues

If your notes aren't syncing across devices:

  • Ensure you're signed in to the same iCloud account on all devices
  • Check that iCloud Drive is enabled in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud
  • Make sure you have enough iCloud storage available
  • Restart the app or your device if sync seems stuck

Notes Not Appearing

If you can't find your notes:

  • Check if the note is in a different category
  • Use the search feature to locate specific notes
  • Make sure you're in the correct view or section

Performance or Freezing

If Jotable seems slow or unresponsive:

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Restart your device
  • Update Jotable to the latest version from the App Store
